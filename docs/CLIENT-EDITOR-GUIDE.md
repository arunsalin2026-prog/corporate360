# Corporate 360 Hub — How to Edit Your Website Content

**Your content editor:** https://corporate360hub.sanity.studio
**Login with:** Your Sanity account email (the one you used when you signed up)

---

## How it works

When you log in to the editor and make changes, you click **Publish**. Your website will automatically update within **60 seconds**. No developers needed, no waiting.

---

## Getting to your editor

1. Open your browser and go to: **https://corporate360hub.sanity.studio**
2. Click **Continue with Google** (or use your email + password if you signed up with email)
3. You'll see a dark sidebar on the left with 5 sections:
   - **Home Page**
   - **About Page**
   - **Services Page**
   - **Careers Page**
   - **Site Settings**
4. Click any section to open it and start editing

> **Important:** Always look for the **Publish** button (blue button, top right corner) after editing. If you only see **Save Draft**, your changes are NOT live yet — you must click **Publish**.

---

## Section 1 — Home Page

This controls the text on your website's front page.

**To edit:**
1. In the left sidebar, click **Home Page**
2. You'll see fields you can type in. Click on any field and start typing.
3. When done, click **Publish** (top right, blue button)

**What you can change:**

| Field | What it controls on the website |
|---|---|
| Hero Headline | The large text at the top of the homepage (e.g., "We Mold Businesses.") |
| Hero Sub-headline | The smaller text just below the headline |
| Hero CTA Button Text | The text on the gold "Let's Build Together" button |
| Intro Paragraph | The body text in the section just below the hero |
| Stat Badge 1, 2, 3 | The 3 small gold stat boxes (e.g., "14+ Districts") |
| Why Us Headline | The headline in the "One Partner. Total Ownership." section |
| Why Us Subtext | The paragraph below that headline |

## Section 2 — About Page

This controls the content on the **About Us** page.

**To edit:**
1. In the left sidebar, click **About Page**
2. Edit the fields below
3. Click **Publish** when done

**What you can change:**

| Field | What it controls |
|---|---|
| Vision | The text in the dark navy "Vision" card |
| Mission | The text in the teal "Mission" card |
| Founder Name | Your name, shown in the Team section |
| Founder Designation | Your title (e.g., "Founder & CEO") |
| Founder Photo | Your photo — see upload instructions below |
| Our Story | The story paragraphs in the "Why We Built This" section |
| Core Values | The 5 navy cards showing your company values (title + description each) |

**How to upload your founder photo:**
1. Click on the **Founder Photo** field
2. Click **Upload** and select your photo from your computer
3. You can drag the dot on the preview to set the crop focus point (useful for portraits — focus on the face)
4. Click **Publish**

> **Photo tip:** Use a portrait photo (taller than wide). Minimum 600px wide. JPG or PNG.

## Section 3 — Services Page

This controls the content in each of the 5 service pillar sections.

**To edit:**
1. In the left sidebar, click **Services Page**
2. You'll see **Service Pillar 1** through **Service Pillar 5** listed
3. Click on any pillar to expand it and edit

**What you can change per pillar:**

| Field | What it controls |
|---|---|
| Service Name | The heading of that pillar section (e.g., "Talent Acquisition & HR Training") |
| Short Description | A brief summary of the service |
| Features | The checklist items in the "What's Included" card — one item per line |

**To add a feature to the checklist:**
1. Scroll to **Features** inside the pillar
2. Click **Add item**
3. Type the feature text
4. Click **Publish**

**To remove a feature:**
1. Hover over the feature item
2. Click the **three dots (⋯)** that appear on the right
3. Click **Remove**
4. Click **Publish**

> **Note:** The in-depth paragraphs (the long body text) for each pillar are part of the design. Contact your developer to change those.

## Section 4 — Careers Page (Job Openings)

This controls the headline, description, and the list of open job roles shown on your Careers page.

**To edit:**
1. In the left sidebar, click **Careers Page**

### Change the page headline or description
- Edit the **Page Headline** field (e.g., "Join the 360° Team")
- Edit the **Page Sub-headline** field (e.g., "Build businesses. Grow careers.")
- Click **Publish**

### Add a new job role
1. Scroll down to the **Open Roles** section
2. Click **Add item**
3. Fill in:
   - **Role Name** — job title (e.g., "Sales Executive")
   - **Role Type** — e.g., Full Time, Part Time, Contract
   - **Location** — e.g., "All 14 Districts" or "Kochi"
   - **Description** — 1–2 sentence description of the role
   - **Currently Hiring** — leave this toggled **ON** (green) to show the role
4. Click **Publish**

### Close/hide a job role (without deleting it)
1. Click on the role you want to hide
2. Find the **Currently Hiring** toggle
3. Click it to turn it **OFF** (grey)
4. Click **Publish**

The role will immediately disappear from the website but is still saved. You can turn it back on any time.

### Edit an existing role
1. Click on the role name to expand it
2. Edit any field
3. Click **Publish**

> **Do NOT delete roles** — just toggle them off. This keeps your history and you can re-activate them later.

## Section 5 — Site Settings

These are settings that appear across the whole website — footer, WhatsApp button, and more.

**To edit:**
1. In the left sidebar, click **Site Settings**
2. Edit the fields below
3. Click **Publish**

**What you can change:**

| Field | Where it appears |
|---|---|
| Phone Number | Footer contact section |
| WhatsApp Number | The green WhatsApp button (bottom-right on every page) |
| Email Address | Footer contact section |
| LinkedIn URL | LinkedIn icon in the footer — paste your full LinkedIn company page URL |
| Instagram URL | Instagram icon in the footer — paste your Instagram profile URL |
| Facebook URL | Facebook icon in the footer |
| YouTube URL | YouTube icon in the footer |
| Footer Tagline | Small text below the logo in the footer |
| Announcement Banner | A yellow notice strip that appears at the very top of every page — leave blank to hide it |

**How to update the WhatsApp number:**
- Enter just the number with country code, no spaces or symbols
- Example: `919876543210` (for +91 98765 43210)

**How to add social media links:**
- Paste the full URL including `https://`
- Example: `https://www.linkedin.com/company/corporate360hub`

## The golden rule — Always click PUBLISH

Every time you make a change, you'll see one of two buttons in the top right:

| Button you see | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Publish** (blue) | Click this — your changes will go live in 60 seconds |
| **Save Draft** | Your changes are saved but NOT live yet. You still need to click Publish |
| **Published** (grey, no action) | Nothing has changed since last publish — you're up to date |

> **If your changes aren't showing on the website after 2 minutes:**
> 1. Go back to the Studio
> 2. Open the section you edited
> 3. Check if there's a **Publish** button — if yes, you forgot to click it
> 4. Click Publish and wait 60 seconds

## What you CANNOT change from the editor

The following require a developer to change:

- Page layout and structure (how sections are arranged)
- Colors, fonts, or the logo
- Adding new pages to the website
- The navigation menu links
- Contact form or careers form behaviour
- The 14-districts map, timeline, or business model page
- Anything that isn't listed in the sections above

---

## Tips for good content

- **Keep headlines short** — 5 to 10 words work best
- **Stat badges** — use format like `14+ Districts` or `100+ Clients` (value + label in one line)
- **Job descriptions** — 1 to 2 sentences. Clear and direct. What the person will do.
- **Core values** — keep titles to 2–4 words; descriptions to 1 sentence
- **Don't use ALL CAPS** in text fields — the design already handles emphasis
